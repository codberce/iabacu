# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in iabacu welcoming and safe for
everyone, regardless of age, experience, identity, disability, appearance,
background, nationality, religion, or technical choices.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while accepting corrections.
- Give actionable feedback and keep discussions focused on the project.
- Respect privacy, attribution, and the rights attached to educational content.
- Take particular care when interacting with students and other young users.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, deliberate
misinformation, publishing private information, and sustained disruption are
not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently
restrict participation when behavior is harmful to the community. Enforcement
decisions should prioritize safety, proportionality, and the privacy of people
reporting an incident.

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's **Security** tab by
choosing **Report a vulnerability** and clearly label the report as a code of
conduct concern. Do not include sensitive details in a public issue. Maintainers
will acknowledge reports as soon as practical and will keep information limited
to the people needed to resolve the concern.

This code applies in all project spaces and when someone officially represents
the project elsewhere.
